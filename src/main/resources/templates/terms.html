Design Codes

The House Designs are coded as follows
HD FBB-XsqmY
Key
HD - House Design
F – Floors (Number of Levels/ Floors)
B – Bedrooms
B – Bathrooms
X – Style, Features and Plot size
50 x 100 plot – A, B, C, D, E, F
75 x 100 plot – G, H, J, K, L, M
100 x 100 plot – N, P, Q, R, S, T
Larger plots – U, V, W, X, Y, Z
sqm – Square Metres

Y - Options
Example
HD 132-A098A – 3 Bedroomed, Single Floor House with 2 Bathrooms fits on 50 x 100 plot covering 98 square metres, option A
HD 243-K295B – 4 Bedroomed, Two Floor House with 3 Bathrooms fits on 75 x 100 plot covering a total of 295 square metres, option B
